About the Book

Using 15 practical, easy-to-follow exercises, you will identify and pursue your best work – and your best attitude. In the book, Stainer helps you to find clues to your own great work, generate new ideas and possibilities quickly, manage your overwhelming workload, locate the sweet spot between what you want to do and what your organization wants you to do, and double the likelihood that you’ll do what you want to do.
